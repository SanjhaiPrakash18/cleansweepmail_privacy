Privacy Policy for CleanSweep Mail

Effective Date: June 27, 2025

CleanSweep Mail (“the Extension”) is a Chrome extension developed by me, Sanjhai Prakash, a solo developer, to help users automatically clean Gmail Promotions, Social, and Spam emails with one click. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use CleanSweep Mail.

1. Information I Collect

Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, I access your basic profile information (name, email address, and profile picture) using OAuth2 authentication.

Gmail Data: With your consent, I access your Gmail account to read email counts and delete emails in the Promotions, Social, and Spam categories. I only request the minimum necessary Gmail API permissions:

  • Read, modify, and compose Gmail messages
  • Access your basic profile and email address

2. How I Use Your Information

  • To display your Gmail statistics (counts of Promotions, Social, and Spam emails)
  • To allow you to delete emails in bulk from specified categories
  • To show your profile information within the extension UI
  • To authenticate your Google account and maintain your session

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your authentication token and email counts are stored locally on your device using Chrome’s storage API.
  • I do not store, transmit, or share your emails, account information, or authentication tokens with any external servers or third parties.
  • All communication with Google APIs is encrypted via HTTPS.
  • I implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • I do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • Your data is only used to provide and improve the core functionality of the extension.

5. User Control and Data Deletion

  • You can revoke the extension’s access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account’s security settings.
  • You may remove the extension from Chrome at any time.
  • To clear locally stored data, use the extension’s sign-out or clear cache features.

6. Children’s Privacy

CleanSweep Mail is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact me and I will take steps to delete such information.

7. Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be posted within the extension and/or on the Chrome Web Store listing page. Please review this policy periodically for any updates.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact me at sanjhaiprakash18@gmail.com.
